Standard Plans

Subscribe to the Red Book for pre-defined standard care plans for conditions and disciplines, that you can customize to your patients. Conditions are added periodically.

Standard plans are comprehensive with much opportunity to customize for your patient. Similar, alternative outcomes and interventions are suggested, so you can select the best fit. In the app, you'll be able to select just the items you want before importing.

Note that Standard Plans expect you will always include the Standard "Assessment & Critical Thinking." Assessment & Critical Thinking content is therefore not repeated in Standard Plans for Conditions.
